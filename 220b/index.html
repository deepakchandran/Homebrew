readme.txt

Guitar as everything is a piece written on the guitar, to be played on the guitar, while sounding nothing like the guitar.


My idea behind this project was to explore ways of using the guitar in an unconventional manner in the modern rock/electronic music context.

The piece is inspired by Radiohead's Identikit. They're known for coming up with off-the-wall sounds on traditional rock instruments while at the same time retaining an original sense of musicality.


Some of the non-guitar sounding effects that are part of this piece include:

-a drum machine

-drone

-drum stutter

-delay

All the effects are written in ChucK
