SequencEar

Audio/Visual Sequencer

SequencEar is a sequencer that takes you on a journey into the blood vessels of arguably the most annoying creature ever thought of, the minion.

Choose the sequence of beats to be played for 4 different timbres in the DNA strand selection menu. Listen to the sequences by colliding with DNA strands in the blood vessel. Also, play around with the red and white blood cells to get different background drone sounds.

Built in Unity + Chuck.
