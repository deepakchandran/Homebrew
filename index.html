The Journey.

readme.txt

For this composition, I was inspired to create a soundscape of what goes on in one's head during a motorbike ride.

From personal experience, the rider often gets lost in the 'groove' of the ride and unfortunately gets brought back to reality through the rash driving of some other commuters. I've tried to encapsulate this sensation of loosing oneself to the moment and being brought back from that feeling in this ~1minute piece.


Few notable events:

- Bike fails to start 3 times. On the fourth time, you're thrown into the head of the rider.

- During the middle of the piece, you're basically lost in the rider's head while the sound of cars passing by try to provide a frame of reference(and also sound cool)

- The crash!


This piece was created using 4 different chuck programs. The main reason for this was so that I could have more control during the mixing process since the section in the middle was getting quite muddled up with a single chuck program.
