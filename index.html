Tron Chicken!

256A - HW# 1

Tron Chicken is an audio visulaizer built using OpenGl and RTAudio. T. Chicken (yes, we're going to call it that) emits bursts of gamma rays whose shape is based on the frequencies present in the input audio.

Do NOT make Tron Chicken angry!!!

Tron Chicken building a shield in front of the attacker

This can be done by whistling the correct frequencies into the micropohone.
